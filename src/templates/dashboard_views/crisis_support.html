☎️ Crisis Support Hotlines

☎️ 988 Lifeline

US 24/7 suicide and crisis support line. Text or call 988 if in distress.

☎️ Crisis Text Line

Text HELLO to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor.

☎️ SAMHSA Helpline

1-800-662-4357 — Confidential information and referral service for mental health and substance abuse.

☎️ Veterans Crisis Line

1-800-273-8255 (Press 1) — Support for veterans in crisis, available 24/7.
